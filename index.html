About Me

Ryan is from Shoreline, Washington. He is currently attending a Trilogy Coding Bootcamp through the University of Washington. He has a dog named Primrose and two rabbits; Amy and Pip. He enjoys frisbee, video games, and spinning fire poi when able to in his "free time". He also enjoys writing in the third person.
